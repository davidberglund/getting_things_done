Alla valbara labels och en beskrivning till dem:

backlog-action - actionable tasks, important but not urgent

todo-action - important _and_ urgent, actionable. Needs additional labels: single or multistep, delegate or assignee/davidberglund

todo-ref - important, urgent, complete for reference (specify for whome): document, write blog post, create other content 

doing - ongoing task

done - completed task
